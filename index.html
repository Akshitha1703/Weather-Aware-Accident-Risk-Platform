Project Overview

The Weather-Aware Accident Risk Platform integrates U.S. accident datasets with live SharePoint-based weather feeds to analyze how environmental factors influence road safety and accident severity across the United States.

Key Insights

  • Over 1 million accidents recorded nationwide.
  • Visibility < 5 miles increases accident likelihood significantly.
  • Rain & drizzle drive a 20–40% increase in collision frequency.
  • Highest accident volumes observed in Texas, Florida, and California.
  • Winter months and Fridays show peak accident trends.

How to Use This Report

Navigation

  • Use the top tabs to move between Overview, Weather Impact, Location Risk, Time Trends, and How to Use.
  • Slicers at the top allow filtering by State, City, Year, and Weather Condition.
  • Hover over charts for detailed tooltips.
  • Click visuals to cross-filter all charts on the page.
  • Use Clear All Slicers to reset the view instantly.

Recommendations

For Stakeholders

  • Deploy predictive AI models for real-time hazard alerts.
  • Optimize road maintenance budgets in high-risk regions.
  • Improve driver education programs based on weather trends.

For Public Safety

  • Reduce speed in low visibility (< 5 miles).
  • Avoid travel during severe thunderstorms or snow.
  • Perform regular vehicle maintenance before winter season.
